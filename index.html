Grammar Chapter 1 - Noun

Definition: A noun is the name of a person, place, animal, thing, or idea.

Examples:
Ali, Karachi, Cat, Book, Love

Types of Noun:

1. Proper Noun = Imad, Pakistan

2. Common Noun = boy, city

3. Collective Noun = team, class

4. Abstract Noun = honesty, beauty

Exercise:
Find nouns in sentence: Ali has a book.

Grammar Chapter 2 - Pronoun

Definition: A pronoun is used instead of a noun.

Examples:
He, She, It, They, We, You

Example Sentence:
Ali is a student. He studies daily.

Types:

Personal Pronoun = I, We

Possessive Pronoun = Mine, Yours

Exercise:
Replace noun with pronoun: Sara is happy. ___ is happy.

Grammar Chapter 3 - Verb

Definition: A verb shows action or state.

Examples:
Run, Eat, Sleep, Write, Is, Are

Sentences:
I run daily.
She is happy.

Types:

Action Verb = play, work

Helping Verb = is, am, are

Exercise:
Find verb: Ali reads a newspaper.

Grammar Chapter 4 - Tenses

Present Indefinite:
I play cricket.

Present Continuous:
I am reading.

Past Indefinite:
I went to school.

Future Indefinite:
I will help you.

Tip: Tense shows time of action.
