📘 Grammar Full Book

Chapter 1: Noun

Definition: A noun is the name of a person, place, animal, thing, or idea.

Examples: Ali, Pakistan, Cat, Table, Honesty

Explanation: Whenever we name something, that word is called a noun.

Types of Noun:

1. Proper Noun = Ali, Karachi

2. Common Noun = boy, city

3. Collective Noun = team, class

4. Abstract Noun = love, beauty

Exercise: Find nouns in sentence: Ali has a book.

Chapter 2: Pronoun

Definition: A pronoun is used instead of a noun.

Examples: He, She, It, They, We

Sentence: Sara is kind. She helps everyone.

Exercise: Replace noun: Ahmed is a student. ___ is a student.

Chapter 3: Verb

Definition: A verb shows action or state.

Examples: Run, Eat, Sleep, Write, Is

Sentence: I play cricket. She is happy.

Types: Action Verb, Helping Verb

Chapter 4: Adjective

Definition: An adjective describes a noun.

Examples: Beautiful girl, Big house, Smart boy

Chapter 5: Adverb

Definition: An adverb describes a verb or adjective.

Examples: Slowly, Quickly, Very

Chapter 6: Tenses Full Detail

Definition: Tense shows the time of an action. It tells whether an action happens in present, past, or future.

1. Present Indefinite Tense

Use: Daily routine, habits, facts.

Structure: Subject + V1 + Object

Examples:

I play cricket.

He goes to school.

2. Present Continuous Tense

Use: Action happening now.

Structure: Subject + is/am/are + V1+ing

Examples:

I am reading.

They are playing.

3. Present Perfect Tense

Use: Completed action.

Structure: Subject + has/have + V3

Examples:

I have finished work.

She has eaten food.

4. Present Perfect Continuous

Use: Action started in past and continuing now.

Structure: Subject + has/have been + V1+ing

Example: I have been studying for two hours.

5. Past Indefinite Tense

Use: Completed action in past.

Structure: Subject + V2

Examples:

I visited Lahore.

He wrote a letter.

6. Past Continuous Tense

Structure: Subject + was/were + V1+ing

Example: I was sleeping.

7. Past Perfect Tense

Structure: Subject + had + V3

Example: I had completed homework.

8. Past Perfect Continuous

Structure: Subject + had been + V1+ing

Example: I had been waiting for one hour.

9. Future Indefinite Tense

Structure: Subject + will + V1

Example: I will help you.

10. Future Continuous Tense

Structure: Subject + will be + V1+ing

Example: I will be studying.

11. Future Perfect Tense

Structure: Subject + will have + V3

Example: I will have finished work.

12. Future Perfect Continuous

Structure: Subject + will have been + V1+ing

Example: I will have been working for two hours.

Practice Exercise

1. He ____ to school daily. (go/goes)

2. I ____ reading now. (am/is)

3. She ____ completed work. (has/have)
