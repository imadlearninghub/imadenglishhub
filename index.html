Basic English Lesson

What is English?
English is an international language used for communication, business, study, and travel. Learning English helps you grow professionally.

Daily Sentences:
How are you?
I am fine.
What is your name?
My name is Imad.

Practice:
Write five sentences about yourself in English.

Grammar Notes

Noun: A noun is the name of a person, place, animal, or thing.
Examples: Ali, Karachi, Cat, Book.

Pronoun: A pronoun is used instead of a noun.
Examples: He, She, It, They.

Verb: A verb shows action.
Examples: Run, Eat, Write.

Exercise:
Find noun and verb in sentence: Ali reads a book.
