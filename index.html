Full Grammar Book

1. Noun

A noun is the name of a person, place, animal, thing, or idea.

Examples: Ali, Karachi, Cat, Book, Love

2. Pronoun

A pronoun is used instead of a noun.

Examples: He, She, It, They, We

3. Verb

A verb shows action or state.

Examples: Run, Eat, Sleep, Is, Are

4. Adjective

An adjective describes a noun.

Examples: Beautiful girl, Big house

5. Adverb

An adverb describes a verb, adjective, or another adverb.

Examples: Slowly, Very, Quickly

6. Preposition

Shows relation of place/time.

Examples: in, on, at, under, behind

7. Conjunction

Joins words or sentences.

Examples: and, but, because

8. Interjection

Expresses sudden feeling.

Examples: Wow! Oh! Alas!

9. Articles

A, An, The

Use a/an for singular countable nouns. Use the for specific nouns.

10. Singular / Plural

Book → Books, Child → Children

11. Tenses Overview

Present, Past, Future (4 forms each = 12 tenses)

12. Present Simple

I play cricket. She goes to school.

13. Present Continuous

I am reading now.

14. Present Perfect

I have completed my work.

15. Past Simple

I went to market yesterday.

16. Future Simple

I will call you tomorrow.

17. Active Voice

Ali wrote a letter.

18. Passive Voice

A letter was written by Ali.

19. Direct Speech

He said, “I am happy.”

20. Indirect Speech

He said that he was happy.

21. Modals

can, could, may, might, should, must

22. Conditionals

If you study, you will pass.

23. Subject Verb Agreement

He goes. They go.

24. Punctuation

Use full stop, comma, question mark correctly.

25. Common Errors

Wrong: He go school.
Correct: He goes to school.
